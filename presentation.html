Data Engineering

10-Step Preprocessing Pipeline

Every input goes through a rigorous engineering chain before the model sees a single number. No shortcuts, no leakage.

01

Raw Data Ingestion

Load Sentinel-5P, MODIS AOD, ERA5 reanalysis, and EAD station CSVs. Aggregate hourly readings to daily means.

pandas / xarray
02

Unit Conversion

NO2 from mol/m2 to ug/m3. AOD scaled by 0.001. Wind speed from m/s to km/h. All values checked for physical plausibility.

Domain calibration
03

Quality Filtering

Remove negative AOD, flag MODIS cloud-contamination, drop station records with QC flags. Clip extreme outliers beyond 5-sigma.

QC / outlier removal
04

Spatial Merge

Left-join all satellite and ERA5 grids onto ground station coordinates via nearest-pixel lookup. One row per (Station, Date).

Geospatial join
05

Lag Feature Engineering

PM2.5 and PM10 lags at 1, 3, 7, 14 days. 7-day and 14-day rolling means. Exponentially weighted moving averages.

Temporal memory
06

Physics Feature Derivation

AOD / BLH ratio (aerosol concentration in breathing layer). Wind U/V decomposition. Ventilation Index. Stability Index.

Atmospheric physics
07

Static Geography Extraction

Elevation from Open-Elevation API. Distance to UAE coastline, E11 highway, and Corniche computed per station. Computed once, stored.

GeoPandas / Shapely
08

Dynamic Regional Persistence (DRP)

Inverse-distance-weighted average of neighbouring station PM readings at 1-day and 3-day lags. Captures regional transport.

Spatial IDW
09

Strict Temporal Split

Train: 2022-2023. Validation: early 2024. Test: late 2024. No shuffling. No future data can leak into training. Hard cutoff.

Zero data leakage
10

Scaling and Serialization

RobustScaler fitted on train only, applied to val and test. Scaler, feature list, and splits saved to disk for reproducibility.

RobustScaler / joblib

Key Findings & Roadmap

What We Learned. Where We Go.

Key Discoveries
01

Temporal Memory Is the Dominant Signal

Adding 1-week PM lags doubled R² in a single experiment. Pollution is persistent; history is the best predictor.

02

Physics Features Outperform Raw Inputs

AOD÷BLH (aerosol concentration in the breathing layer) ranked #1 in SHAP. Science-informed features beat blind engineering.

03

GBMs Win on Structured Data

LSTM produced near-zero R². LightGBM with Optuna beats all deep learning architectures on this dataset.

04

Regional Pollution Transport Matters

DRP features, IDW-weighted readings from neighbouring stations, capture smoke and dust transport that local sensors miss.
